- AI for Science

**Changjun Fan** is an Associate Professor at the College of Systems Engineering, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). His research focuses on complex networks, deep graph learning, and combinatorial optimization, with applications to AI for Science and decision intelligence.

## Education

- Ph.D., National University of Defense Technology (NUDT)
- Visiting PhD Student, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), 2017 – 2019, advised by Prof. Yizhou Sun
- B.S., National University of Defense Technology (NUDT)

## Experience

- 2022 – present: **Associate Professor**, College of Systems Engineering, NUDT
- 2020 – 2022: **Assistant Professor**, College of Systems Engineering, NUDT

## Research Interests

- Complex network analysis, network dismantling, network resilience
- Machine learning for combinatorial optimization
- AI for Science
- Decision intelligence

## Honors & Awards

1. **First Prize**, Science and Technology Progress Award, China Simulation Society, 2025 (10/15)
2. **First Prize**, CICC Scientific and Technological Progress Award, Chinese Institute of Command and Control, 2024 (6/12)
3. **Best Paper Award**, IEEE SmartData, 2024 (Corresponding author)
4. **First Prize**, Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Science and Technology Progress Award, 2023 (12/15)
5. **WAIC Youth Outstanding Paper Award**, World Artificial Intelligence Conference, 2023 (1/4)
6. Science and Technology Innovation Program of Hunan Province, 2023
7. **First Prize**, Youth Innovation Award, NUDT, 2022 (1/1)

## Grants (Principal Investigator)

1. **NSFC General Project** (2026 – 2028): Research on Key Technologies of Temporal Knowledge Graphs under Incomplete Information
2. **NSFC Project for Young Scholars** (2023 – 2025): Processing Structural Similarity Queries on Large-Scale Graph Data
3. **Hunan Provincial NSF Project for Young Scholars** (2015 – 2017): Frequent Subgraph Mining in Single Massive Graphs

## Service

- **Deputy Secretary-General**, Information Fusion Committee, Chinese Institute of Command and Control
- Reviewer for KDD, WWW, ICDM, ICDE, *Physica A*, and others
