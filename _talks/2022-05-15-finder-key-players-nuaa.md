<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

应南京航空航天大学天地一体频谱认知实验室「认知决策机理与智能科学基础理论高端论坛」邀请所作的学术报告，围绕复杂网络中关键参与者的识别问题，介绍了团队提出的 **FINDER** 框架——基于深度强化学习（Q-学习 + 图嵌入），在小规模网络上训练后即可高效泛化到大规模网络，发现对网络连通性影响最大的关键节点。

相关报道：[南京航空航天大学](https://cog.nuaa.edu.cn/2022/0516/c754a282871/page.htm)

</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

Invited talk at the "High-end Forum on Cognitive Decision-Making and Foundational Theories of Intelligent Science," hosted by the Lab of Integrated Spectrum Cognition at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). The talk addressed the identification of key players in complex networks and introduced our **FINDER** framework, which combines deep reinforcement learning (Q-learning with graph embedding): trained on small networks, it generalizes efficiently to large-scale networks to discover the nodes most critical to network connectivity.

Reference: [NUAA report](https://cog.nuaa.edu.cn/2022/0516/c754a282871/page.htm)

</div>
