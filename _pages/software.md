## FINDER

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

**FINDER**（*FInding key players in complex Networks through DEep Reinforcement learning*）：基于深度强化学习的复杂网络关键节点识别框架。仅需在小规模网络上训练，即可高效地泛化到大规模网络，用于网络瓦解与关键节点发现。

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/FINDER) · [论文](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-020-0177-2)（*Nature Machine Intelligence*, 2020）

</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

**FINDER** (*FInding key players in complex Networks through DEep Reinforcement learning*): a deep reinforcement learning framework for finding key players in complex networks. Trained only on small-scale graphs, it generalizes efficiently to large-scale networks for network dismantling and key-node discovery.

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/FINDER) · [Paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-020-0177-2) (*Nature Machine Intelligence*, 2020)

</div>

## DIRAC

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

**DIRAC**（*Deep reinforcement learning for spIn-glass gRound-stAte Calculation*）：用于自旋玻璃基态计算的深度强化学习框架。可在小规模实例上训练并应用于任意大规模实例，具有良好的可扩展性，并能增强各类热退火方法。

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/DIRAC) · [论文](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-023-36363-w)（*Nature Communications*, 2023）

</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

**DIRAC** (*Deep reinforcement learning for spIn-glass gRound-stAte Calculation*): a deep reinforcement learning framework for computing spin-glass ground states. It can be trained on small-scale instances and applied to arbitrarily large ones with strong scalability, and can be leveraged to enhance any thermal annealing method.

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/DIRAC) · [Paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-023-36363-w) (*Nature Communications*, 2023)

</div>

## DrBC

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

**DrBC**（*Deep ranker for Betweenness Centrality*）：基于图神经网络的介数中心性节点排序模型，采用编码器–解码器结构端到端学习，可高效识别图中高介数中心性的关键节点。

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/DrBC) · [论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.10418)（*CIKM*, 2019）

</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

**DrBC** (*Deep ranker for Betweenness Centrality*): a graph-neural-network ranking model for betweenness centrality. With an end-to-end encoder–decoder architecture, it efficiently identifies high-betweenness-centrality nodes in large graphs.

[GitHub](https://github.com/FFrankyy/DrBC) · [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.10418) (*CIKM*, 2019)

</div>
